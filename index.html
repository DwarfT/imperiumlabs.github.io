Our Games

The games we are currently working on at Imperium Interactice.

In the Beginning

In the Beginning is a game where you take the role of god. After selecting some initial options, In the Beginning will procedurally generate your very own world. Through your own interactions you can spark the seed of life. In the Beginning will then simulate life evolving into civilizations that spread across the world. Imagine being able to look down on humanity, grab a lightning bolt from the sky and chuck it at them. Being able to walk away from your planet, grab a meteor, and also chuck it at your planet. Being able to come down to earth as a giant. Being worshipped. Omnipotence. The world is at your fingertips.

Empire

Empire is an early development text-based MMORPG with a focus on encouraging complex player interaction. The core of the game revolves around player-created groups, and the inevitable alliances and rivalries that are formed on the path of expanding your empire and acquiring wealth. Through emergent gameplay design and unscripted player-driven competition, we want our players to experience epic stories of betrayal, warfare, politics, and things that we at Imperium Interactive can not even begin to imagine. The game is set in an amalgamation of medieval Europe and Japan, and features variable procedural terrain across the entirety of the map.
